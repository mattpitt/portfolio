Christmas and New Years Offer


Clients: Sunrise Medical, AMP Magazine


Sunrise Medical typically use lifestyle and product images for their campaigns, but I chose to create an illustration for one of their limited Christmas and New Years offer. This achieved the goal of grabbing attention, as I was then asked to transfer the design to an industry magazine.



I was first asked to create a Christmas card for a wheelchair with a “lift” feature. My idea was to add sentiment by showing the lift feature help the user put a tree on a star. This then turned into a new years gift and was used for print and digital marketing collateral.
