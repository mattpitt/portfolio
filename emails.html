Collection of Email Designs


Main clients: Bolle, Goretex, Kask, Enertor


During my time with Beeswift, I designed in-house emails for established companies such as 3M, Dupont, Bolle and more. The ESP I used was Sendinblue, which had a drag and drop email builder. Over time I worked out ways to create more fluid and professional designs, such as adding gradients to blend images in with sections.



I eventually learnt email HTML and CSS and wrote an email for Kask helmets from scratch. This allowed more customisation of layout, rather than using default sections provided by Sendinblue.
