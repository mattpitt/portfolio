World Book Day - Reading Encouragement


Clients: Literacy Trust - Nottingham Trent University


An open live brief which aimed to encourage children to read on World Book Day. My goal was to utilise a method of learning that was hands on, so after researching different artistic activities, I designed origami animals to tell stories. This would then have developed into a learning app and a goodie bag if the concept went through.
